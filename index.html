Andreia Constantino . 1990 . Portugal

Andreia holds a degree in Communication Design from the Faculty of Fine Arts in Lisbon (2011). She followed up this cycle of studies with a master's degree in Communication Design and New Media at the same institution. The decision to "give it some time" was made to give way to a new adventure: Collectivo 4.16 - with 3 university colleagues she developed the first graphic projects, one of them was awarded in 2014 in the Young Creators Contest. Between 2012-14, she was in charge of space management, cultural program and product selection at Fabrica Features Lisboa - Benetton Group. As a designer, she grew up in Studio AH-HA (2014-17), in which she learned almost everything she knows today!

In recent months, Andreia has sat at the table with the Multifood Group, where she has developed identities that will have you eating and begging for more. Proud to be part of a fierce and enthusiastic generation, Andreia has simultaneously developed projects on a freelancer basis for friends looking to give shape to good ideas!
