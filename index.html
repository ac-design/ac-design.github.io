Andreia Constantino
—

Graduated in Communication Design
from the Faculty of Fine Arts in Lisbon, 2011.

The adventure started as Colectivo 4.16 – along with 3 university colleagues she developed her first graphic projects, which led her to an award in 2014 in the Young Creators Contest. Between 2012-14, Andreia was in charge of space management and product selection at Fabrica Features Lisboa – Benetton Group.

As a graphic designer, she grew up at Studio AH-HA (2014-17), where she had a great time creating holistic visual solutions and working through every stage of the process. Over the last year, Andreia worked with Multifood Group, developing identities that will have you 'eating and begging for more'.

Currently she’s around the cultural scene as resident designer at Calouste Gulbenkian Foundation. Proud to be part of a fierce and enthusiastic generation, Andreia is simultaneously developing projects for friends on a freelancer basis, always seeking to shape good ideas!
